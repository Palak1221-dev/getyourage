# How to Calculate Exact Age for School Admission

Enrolling a child in school, daycare, or youth sports leagues often requires knowing their exact age on a specific cutoff date. "Close enough" doesn't work when districts have strict age requirements down to the day.

## The Importance of Cutoff Dates

School districts typically enforce a hard cutoff date (e.g., September 1st). If a child turns five on September 2nd, they may have to wait an entire year to begin Kindergarten. 

When filling out admission paperwork, administrators will ask for the child's exact age in years and months as of that specific cutoff date, not as of the day you fill out the form.

## Sports Leagues and "League Age"

Youth sports leagues (like Little League Baseball or youth soccer) also use specific age cutoff dates to determine which division a child plays in. This is often referred to as their "League Age." Using our date calculators ensures you register your child in the correct, safe, and legally compliant division.
