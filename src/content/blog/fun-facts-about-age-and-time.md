# Fun Facts About Age and Time

Time is a relentless force, and age is how we measure our journey through it. While our [Exact Age Calculator](/) gives you precise data on your personal timeline, looking at age and time from a broader perspective reveals some mind-bending realities.

## 1. You Are Older Than You Think (Biologically)
In many cultures, specifically in parts of East Asia like South Korea and China (traditionally), a person is considered one year old at birth, and they age a year on the New Year. While digital calculators use the Western standard of starting at zero, biological age is complex. Your cells have been developing since conception!

## 2. A "Billion Second" Milestone
Measuring life in seconds gives you a massive number very quickly. Did you know that you celebrate your one-billionth second of life when you are exactly 31 years, 251 days, 13 hours, 34 minutes, and 54 seconds old? It’s a milestone worth throwing a party for.

## 3. Time Speeds Up As You Age
Psychologists have noted that as we age, our perception of time accelerates. When you are 5 years old, one year represents 20% of your entire life. When you are 50, one year represents just 2% of your life. This proportional theory explains why childhood summers felt endless, while adult years seem to fly by.

## 4. You Share a Birthday With Millions
With over 8 billion people on Earth, you share your exact birthday (month and day) with roughly 22 million other people. 

## 5. The Oldest Recorded Human
The longest verified human lifespan belongs to Jeanne Calment of France, who lived to be 122 years and 164 days old. If you want to see how many days that is, plug her lifespan into our duration calculator!
